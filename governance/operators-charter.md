# The Operator's Charter (v1.0)
*Our public, permanent commitments. Versioned; changes announced 30 days
ahead. If we break a line here, point at it.*

**§1 Honest odds before you pay.** Most new stores fail (industry estimates:
80–90% in the first months). We say this on the pricing page and in signup —
before any card number — in readable type, never fine print. We publish user
outcomes yearly: % reaching profitability, median profit of active stores,
median loss of closed ones. Real numbers, including bad ones.

**§2 We never promise income.** No income figures, no "passive income /
guaranteed / proven system / get rich," no lifestyle imagery as implied
results, no top-performer stories without typical results shown adjacent in
equal type. Applies to us AND anyone promoting us.

**§3 Picks are never for sale.** No supplier or brand can pay into your
recommendations, ever. Scores come from market data alone. Any future
sponsored placement is visually distinct, labeled "Sponsored," and never
mixed into ranked picks. Pick distribution/saturation rules are disclosed;
"exclusive" is never claimed unless enforced by code.

**§4 Your data is never used against you.** We never operate competing
retail stores (company, subsidiaries, or staff side businesses — staff
attest annually). Individual store data is never sold, shared, or shown to
others. Benchmarks: aggregated, anonymized, minimum pool sizes, opt-in with
no service penalty. Leave anytime with export + deletion honored.

**§5 No manipulation tools.** Never in this product: fake countdown timers,
fake stock scarcity, fake reviews or imported ones, fake purchase popups.
Urgency comes from true facts or doesn't exist.

**§6 Real money always feels like real money.** Hard spending caps enforced
in the execution layer; plain-dollar confirmations above your threshold; no
streaks, confetti, or reward mechanics tied to spending — celebration is for
learning milestones and verified profit.

**§7 We tell you when to stop.** The coach flags sustained losses plainly,
including "pause or stop" when warranted; helps you wind down cleanly at no
charge; never answers your losses with an upsell. "Quit with your money
intact" is a respected outcome here.

**§8 Honest AI.** Every pick shows confidence + evidence. We publish the
engine's hit rate quarterly, by a public method, whatever the number. The AI
is a research assistant, not an oracle — it will be wrong regularly, and the
product says so.

**§9 Fair, boring billing.** Full price on the page; no credits or surprise
add-ons; cancel in two clicks; email before every renewal/trial conversion;
existing subscribers keep their price ≥6 months after changes; no fee ever
profits from your losses (no refund fees).

**§10 Protection comes standard.** Dispute radar, ad pre-checks, category
safety filters, tax alerts — included in every plan. Safety is never
paywalled.

**§11 Accountability.** Report violations to charter@[domain] — a human
reads every one. Annual transparency report: outcomes (§1), hit rates (§8),
charter reports + actions. This charter is incorporated into our Terms and
reviewed by counsel.

*Signed by the founder at launch. If we can't run this business under these
rules, we don't deserve to run it.*
